## One Insight

The physical therapy and chiropractic vertical is the most compelling walk-in pitch in this market for one reason: the pain point is clinical, not just operational. A PT practice owner knows that a patient who drops out at session 6 of 16 didn't get a good outcome. The practice failed them. That's not just a revenue problem — it's a clinical quality problem, and it bothers owners in a way that "you're leaving money on the table" doesn't always land.

Lead with that. "The patients who drop out at session 5 when they feel better are the ones who come back in 8 months with a worse version of the same injury. Your compliance system isn't just about revenue — it's about outcomes." Owners who care about their patients respond to that framing immediately. The revenue math is the close, not the opener.
