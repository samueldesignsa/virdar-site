### 4. Auto repair shop — Irving / Story Road or Pioneer Pkwy
**Area:** Story Road near Belt Line, or Pioneer Pkwy in the Irving section heading toward Grand Prairie — dense auto repair corridor.
**What they do:** Independent auto repair — brakes, tires, oil change, diagnostics.
**What I noticed:** July 4 weekend means 300–500 mile road trips for a lot of Irving families heading to West Texas, the Hill Country, or Oklahoma. A shop with declined brake or tire recommendations from March or April has customers planning to make that drive. The shop that reaches those customers this week — "you're driving somewhere this weekend and your brake pads were marginal last time you were in — want us to check them before you go?" — books that job. The shop that doesn't loses the job to the chain that happens to be on the route.
**Hook:** "Do you track declined services? When a customer says 'not today' on brakes or tires, where does that go? If it goes nowhere, you're sending people home for July 4 weekend on tires you know are borderline."
**Timing:** Get here before 8am — owner is usually in, phones are quiet, crew hasn't rolled out yet.
