# LinkedIn Post — Monday, May 11, 2026

**Topic:** The renewal gap — independent service businesses already know exactly when each client is at risk, but they let the moment pass without reaching out first.

**Post for today (Monday). Best window: 7–9am CT or 11am–1pm CT.**

---

Your insurance agency knows exactly when every client is due for renewal. Your auto shop knows every vehicle's next service interval. Your dental practice knows every patient's 6-month recall date.

Almost none of them use that information to reach out before the client gets a notice from somewhere else.

Here's what happens when you don't: the client gets a renewal declaration in the mail, sees the premium went up $340, and for 48 hours they're thinking about shopping. If a competitor's postcard or a neighbor's recommendation appears during those 48 hours, they make a call. If your agency calls first — before the notice lands — there's no shopping window. The conversation already happened.

The same dynamic plays out in auto repair, dental, pest control, and any business where you know in advance when a client is due back.

This is not a marketing problem. The data is already there. The trigger date is already known. The only thing missing is the outreach that happens automatically, 45 days before the window opens.

Businesses that run this are not doing anything complicated. They're just not leaving their client base on passive.
