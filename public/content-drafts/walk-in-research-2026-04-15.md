# Walk-In Research — Tuesday, April 15, 2026

**Target Zone:** Preston Hollow / Northwest Dallas / Forest Lane corridor
**Focus:** Home service businesses (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, pest control) serving high-income residential neighborhoods
**Why this zone:** Preston Hollow and the surrounding neighborhoods are among Dallas's highest-income residential areas. The home service businesses that work this market handle higher average tickets ($500–$1,500 per job), more complex jobs, and homeowners who expect fast, professional service. When they can't reach a contractor quickly, they call someone else — and they rarely come back. The missed-call cost here is higher than almost anywhere else in the metro.
