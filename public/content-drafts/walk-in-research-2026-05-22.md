## Target 1: HVAC Company — Grand Prairie or Arlington

**What they do:** Independent HVAC company. Service, repair, and installation. Residential and light commercial. Owner-operated or small family business (3–15 employees), not a Trane Comfort Specialist franchise or major chain.

**Where to look:** Google Maps — "HVAC company Grand Prairie TX" or "air conditioning repair Arlington TX." The SH-360 corridor in Grand Prairie and the eastern Arlington commercial area along Arkansas Ln and Pioneer Pkwy have independent HVAC shops. Many operate out of light industrial spaces or small commercial units rather than storefront retail — look for a unit with trucks in the parking lot. In Arlington, Cooper St between I-30 and I-20 has clusters of independent trade businesses.

**What to notice:** This is the best week of the year to walk into an HVAC company. Summer starts now in DFW. May-June-July is when equipment installs and emergency service calls spike. A company that's heading into summer without a systematic lead response system, outstanding spring quotes, or a seasonal outreach campaign is leaving money on the table at the exact moment it's most available.

Check their Google reviews: how many reviews have a response from the owner? An HVAC company with 120 reviews and 5 responses has an obvious gap. Also check the recency of their reviews — a company doing 80+ service calls a month with only 2 reviews in the last 60 days is not asking.

**Hook:** "I work with HVAC companies on one problem: the leads and quotes that don't get systematic follow-up. You wrote spring tune-up quotes in March and April. For the homeowners who haven't called back yet — what does your follow-up look like after the first call? Because the window on those quotes is closing, and the ones that convert over the next 30 days are usually the ones someone followed up with twice."

**Revenue math:** A company with 18 outstanding equipment quotes at $10,500 average, converting 2 more through a three-touch follow-up sequence, adds $21,000 from quotes already scoped. Seasonal outreach to 500 existing customers at 10% tune-up conversion adds $9,000 in a single campaign.
