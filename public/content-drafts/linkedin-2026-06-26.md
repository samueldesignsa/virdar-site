**Target:** Business owners, operators, independent service providers in DFW
**Theme:** July 4 cancellations — the problem you can prevent right now

July 4 is next Friday.

Right now, your patients and clients are finalizing travel plans. Some of them have appointments scheduled with you the week of July 7. Some of them will cancel July 3. A few will just not show.

The practices that protect that week send a message this week — not a reminder, a message.

"Confirming your Tuesday July 7 appointment. If you'll be traveling for the holiday, reply and we'll move it to a time that works. We have openings the week of July 14."

That message does two things:
- It gives the patient who was going to cancel a path that doesn't lose you the slot
- It identifies the cancellations early enough to fill them

The appointment that cancels July 3 at 5pm is gone. The appointment that rescheduled July 2 at noon is revenue that stayed.

One message. Sent this week. Protects the whole calendar.
