Shops that are proactively reaching customers about summer safety checks are converting those conversations. Shops that aren't are seeing customers walk in already primed to shop on price.

**Hook:** "The declined service follow-up is the easiest and fastest revenue recovery in auto repair. The customer who said 'not today' on the bearings in April — they didn't fix it.
