## Target Businesses

### 1. Any Independent Roofing Contractor — Addison / North Dallas
**What they do:** Residential and commercial roof installation, repair, and insurance claim work  
**Address:** Check Google Maps for "roofing contractor Addison TX" or "roofing company near Midway Rd Dallas" — there are a half-dozen independents within 5 miles  
**What to notice:** Most have no online scheduling. Estimate follow-up is manual and inconsistent. Post-storm lead volume overwhelms office staff.  
**Hook:** "What happens to estimates you send out when the homeowner goes quiet after two weeks? Do you have a follow-up process, or does it depend on someone remembering to call?"  
**Leave-behind:** virdar.co/blog/ai-automation-for-roofing-contractors.html  
**Best time:** 8–10am before crews head out and dispatch gets chaotic

### 2. Addison Insurance Agency (Midway Rd / Belt Line area)
**What they do:** Independent P&C insurance agency — auto, home, commercial  
**Address:** Look for independent agencies (not State Farm, Allstate storefronts) along Midway Rd or Addison Rd  
**What to notice:** Most independent agencies have no automated renewal follow-up. Clients lapse because the agent never called. Cross-sell is inconsistent.  
**Hook:** "When does a client hear from you between renewals? If the honest answer is 'when we remember,' that's the gap we close."  
**Leave-behind:** virdar.co/blog/ai-automation-for-insurance-agencies.html  
**Best time:** 9–11am

### 3. North Dallas CPA / Accounting Firm (Preston Rd or Midway Rd)
**What they do:** Tax prep and accounting for small businesses and individuals  
**Address:** Office parks along Preston Rd between LBJ and Arapaho, or near the Galleria  
**What to notice:** Tax season just ended. Off-season client communication is almost universally nonexistent. Post-season is the perfect window — owners have mental bandwidth and fresh pain.  
**Hook:** "What does your off-season outreach look like? Most firms I talk to say they don't really have one. That's the clients who drift away."  
**Leave-behind:** virdar.co/blog/ai-automation-for-accounting-cpa-firms.html  
**Best time:** 10am–12pm (post-morning rush, before lunch)

### 4. Independent Auto Repair Shop — Addison / Farmers Branch
**What they do:** General auto repair, oil changes, diagnostics  
**Address:** Google "independent auto repair Addison TX" or "auto shop Farmers Branch" — Marsh Lane and Forest Lane have several  
**What to notice:** Most have no systematic declined service follow-up. Customer who declines a brake job in March is never contacted in April. Lapsed customers (no visit in 18+ months) are invisible.  
**Hook:** "When a customer declines a service and leaves — brakes, tires, whatever — does anyone follow up with them two weeks later? Most shops lose that job to whoever does call."  
**Leave-behind:** virdar.co/blog/ai-automation-for-auto-repair-shops.html  
**Best time:** 8–10am (shop owners are in before the bays fill)

### 5. North Dallas Chiropractic or Physical Therapy Practice (Preston / Midway)
**What they do:** Outpatient chiropractic or PT care  
**Address:** Medical office buildings along Preston Rd or Arapaho Rd in North Dallas  
**What to notice:** Most don't have automated appointment reminders or treatment plan drop-out detection. A patient who misses two appointments is usually gone — and nobody calls them.  
**Hook:** "What happens when a patient goes two weeks without scheduling? Does anyone reach out, or do they just fall off the schedule?"  
**Leave-behind:** virdar.co/blog/ai-automation-for-physical-therapy-chiropractic.html  
**Best time:** 9–11am (before the mid-morning patient rush)

### 6. Independent Optometry Practice — Addison or North Dallas

**Address:** Strip centers along Belt Line Rd in Addison, or Midway Rd  
**What to notice:** Annual recall is the weak spot. Patients who haven't been in 14+ months are invisible unless the practice is running automated outreach. Most aren't.  
**Hook:** "How do you handle annual recall? When a patient is due for an exam, does something go out automatically, or is it manual?"

## Route Suggestion
Start south in the Belt Line / Addison Road core (roofing, insurance, optometry), then work north on Midway toward LBJ (auto repair, PT, accounting). Full circuit is under 10 miles.

## Logistics
- **Park once:** Lot near Addison Town Center or the Addison Athletic Club area — walkable to several businesses
- **Best window:** 8am–12pm. Addison gets busy at lunch and decision-makers often leave for appointments in the afternoon
- **Dress:** Business casual. Addison skews professional.
- **Carry:** Business cards, phone (for URLs), and a note on the new roofing post if hitting any roofers early
