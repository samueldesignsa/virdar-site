## Today's Targets

**July 4 is 10 days out.** Any appointment-based business today hears the same frame: "Your patients who are planning to be out of town are going to cancel on you July 3. The practices that reach them this week — 'Confirming your July appointment, or if you'll be away, we have availability July 7–9' — keep that revenue. The ones that don't eat the cancellations."
