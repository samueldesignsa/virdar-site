Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Virdar ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Book a strategy call — your name, email, and any information you provide via Calendly (processed by Calendly, Inc.).
  • Submit a careers form — your name, email, location, skills, and any other details you enter. This data is processed by Formspree, Inc. and delivered to us via email.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Calendly — for scheduling strategy calls. Subject to Calendly's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree — for processing form submissions. Subject to Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — for loading web fonts. Google may collect anonymized usage data. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Spline — for loading 3D visual elements. Subject to Spline's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — for hosting. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, or tracking pixels on this website. We do not track your browsing behavior.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or schedule a strategy call.
  • Evaluate career interest submissions.
  • Communicate with you about our services if you have requested it.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing info@virdar.co.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at info@virdar.co.
